Flappy Postman

A side-on endless runner where a rattled postman tears through the street, the world scrolls on its own, and one mistimed jump sends him straight into suburban chaos.

One-Button Run

The route auto-scrolls. The only thing you control is the jump, so the timing has to be clean.

Street Obstacles

Dogs, prams, post boxes, cats, holly bushes, bollards, benches, and other pavement hazards keep coming from the right.

Level Ramp

Every 10 cleared objects bumps the level and nudges the speed up, starting mild and getting sharper over time.
