Flappy Postman

A side-on endless runner where a rattled postman tears through the street, the world scrolls on its own, and Carl is trying to recover the PDAs he has scattered across the route before suburban chaos catches up with him.

One-Button Run

The route auto-scrolls. The only thing you control is the jump, so the timing has to be clean.

Lost PDAs

Coins float above the pavement, but every so often one of Carl's lost PDAs appears instead and becomes the real target.

Level Ramp

Every 10 cleared objects bumps the level and nudges the speed up, starting mild and getting sharper over time.

Help Carl find all the PDAs he has lost before his shift is over.

Flappy Postman

Tap the screen or press Up to start. Carl needs to recover his lost PDAs, which appear rarely instead of coins. Hold briefly for extra lift, then tap again midair for the bus-clearing second jump.

Controls: Tap or Up Arrow to jump. Hold for a fraction longer to get a slightly higher arc, then tap again while airborne for the bigger second leap. Use Space, Enter, or the button to restart a run.
